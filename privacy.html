Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Vanguard Daily ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it. We have written this in plain language deliberately — legal jargon serves lawyers, not readers.

1. What We Collect

Vanguard Daily is a read-only news platform. We do not require account creation, login, or registration. As a result, we collect very little:

  • Language preference — stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device.
  • Contact form submissions — if you use our contact form, your name, email, and message are sent directly to our editorial email via your own mail client. We do not operate a server that stores this data.
  • Notification email — if you sign up for advertising launch notifications, your email is stored locally in your browser only. We do not transmit it to any server.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking.
  • We do not use third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel).
  • We do not serve behavioural advertising.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any reader data.
  • We do not collect IP addresses, device fingerprints, or browsing history.

3. Third-Party Services

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log the request to serve the font file. We use Pollinations.ai to generate article cover images — image prompts are sent to their API as part of our editorial pipeline, not by your browser. Article content is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard HTTP access logs.

4. Your Rights

Because we hold no personal data about you on our servers, there is nothing to request deletion of. Your only stored preference (language choice) can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or by using your browser's built-in "Clear site data" function.

5. Children's Privacy

Vanguard Daily does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Our content is intended for a general adult audience.

6. Changes to This Policy

We will update this page and the "Last updated" date above if our practices change. We will not implement data collection practices without updating this policy first.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via our contact page or email us directly at vangurddaily@gmail.com.
