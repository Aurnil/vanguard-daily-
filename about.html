About Us

Independent Journalism,
Two Languages, One Mission

Vanguard Daily is an independent Bangladeshi news platform that delivers rigorous, fact-based reporting in both English and Bangla. We believe every reader — regardless of language preference — deserves access to the same quality of news and analysis.

Who We Are

Founded with a simple conviction: that quality journalism should not be gated by language. Vanguard Daily is built by a small team of journalists, technologists, and editors based in Bangladesh, covering stories that matter locally and globally.

Our bilingual approach is not an afterthought — every story is written and edited in both English and Bangla with equal care. We do not machine-translate. We write for both audiences from the start.

What We Cover

We cover five areas of sustained focus: Politics, Economy, World Affairs, Technology, and Sports. Our editorial priorities are guided by public interest, not traffic metrics.

Our Technology

Vanguard Daily uses an AI-assisted editorial pipeline powered by the Gemini and Groq APIs to help our team process and contextualise a high volume of global and local news sources. Every published article is reviewed by a human editor before it goes live. AI helps us work faster — it does not replace editorial judgement.

Our Independence

Vanguard Daily is not affiliated with any political party, government body, or corporate conglomerate. We are funded entirely through reader support and editorial partnerships. Our reporting is not for sale.

Editorial Standards

  • We correct errors promptly and transparently.
  • We cite sources and link to original reporting where possible.
  • We distinguish clearly between news and analysis.
  • We do not publish rumour or unverified claims.

For editorial inquiries, corrections, or press accreditation, please contact us.
